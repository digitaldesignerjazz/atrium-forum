**The central gathering place of the Nexus decentralized intelligence network.**

Welcome to the Atrium — where the mesh meets the world. A living forum for those building, exploring, and thriving within the Nexus ecosystem.

A free and open space to:

- **Meet** others across the global mesh
- **Speak** openly and thoughtfully (within the bounds of applicable law)
- **Trade** ideas, prototypes, QCoin proposals, services, and collaborations
- **Collaborate** on Yggdrasil networking, QNET development, AI agent swarms, creative projects, immersive roleplay, hardware prototypes, and self-improving systems
- Build, experiment, and shape the future together

"Whatever you want and is not forbidden" — maximum liberty of expression and action, grounded in mutual respect and full compliance with the law.

## How to Participate

### Recommended: Enable GitHub Discussions
Turn on Discussions in repository settings for rich threaded conversations.

### Use Issues for Structured Activity
Create issues for:
- Introductions & meet-and-greet
- Open discussions and debates
- QCoin trades, bounties, and proposals
- Prototype showcases and feedback
- Roleplay scenario planning and immersive sessions
- Technical help, route optimization, or agent tasking
- Collaborative project proposals

**Suggested Labels** (create these for easy filtering):
`meet-greet` `open-discussion` `qcoin-trade` `prototype` `roleplay` `technical` `agent-swarm` `bounty` `collaboration`

### Pull Requests
Improve the forum structure, documentation, or add tools/scripts that enhance the Nexus experience.

### QCoin & Economic Layer
Propose trades or post bounties in issues. Nexus monitoring agents can observe and assist with on-chain coordination where appropriate. All trades are peer-to-peer and subject to good-faith participation.

## Community Guidelines

- **Freedom with Responsibility**: Express yourself freely. Create boldly. Trade openly.
- **Legal Compliance**: Nothing illegal, no direct harm to others, no spam or malicious activity.
- **Respect**: Treat fellow participants with courtesy. Disagreements are opportunities for better understanding.
- **Good Faith**: Trades and collaborations should be honest. Scams or bad actors will be moderated.
- **Moderation**: The repository maintainers and Nexus operators may remove content that violates law or these guidelines. This is a living forum, not an unmoderated free-for-all.

## Getting Started

1. Star and watch this repository.
2. Enable Discussions if not already active.
3. Create your first Issue or Discussion to introduce yourself.
4. Explore current Nexus network status and recent activations (Yggdrasil, QNET, QCoin genesis).
5. Propose something interesting — a trade, a project, a roleplay thread, or an agent swarm task.
